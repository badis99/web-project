WHO ARE WE

More Than
Just Acting

Theatro INSAT is the official theater club of INSAT. Founded in 2003 by passionate students, it provides a creative space where art meets innovation. The club brings together young talents who share a love for acting, directing, and stage performance, offering them opportunities to express themselves and showcase their creativity.

Over the years, Theatro INSAT has expanded into a dynamic structure with several key departments: Acting, Writing, Music, Dance and Media. Through plays, workshops, and cultural events, it has become a vibrant community that celebrates artistic expression and teamwork.

OUR DEPARTMENTS

5 Departments

ACTING

The Acting Department is where creativity meets performance — a space to explore voice, movement, and character while bringing stories to life on stage and screen.

MUSIC

The Music Department is where passion meets rhythm ,a place to create, perform, and bring harmony to life.

DANCING

The Dancing Department is where movement becomes art, a space to express, connect, and tell stories through rhythm and motion.

WRITING

The Writing Department is where ideas turn into powerful stories, a space to craft words, explore creativity, and give voice to imagination.

MEDIA

The Media Department transforms moments into stories, the stage for visual storytelling.

WHO ARE WE

More Than
Just Acting

Founded in 2003, Theatro INSAT has been the creative heartbeat of our college. We are a community of passionate storytellers, dreamers, and artists who believe in the transformative power of theatre.

From classic Shakespeare to contemporary pieces, from intimate monologues to grand productions — we explore every facet of the dramatic arts with dedication and excellence.

OUR TEAM

5 Departments

ACTING

The heart of our club. Our acting department brings stories to life through powerful performances, training members in method acting, improvisation, and stage presence.

MUSIC

From original compositions to live accompaniment, our music department sets the emotional tone for every production, blending classical and contemporary sounds.

DANCE

Movement and expression merge in our dance department, where choreography brings another dimension to our theatrical storytelling.

WRITING

From scriptwriting to direction, our creative minds craft the narratives that drive our productions, adapting classics and creating original works.

MEDIA

Lights, sound, and stage management — our technical team is the backbone that makes the magic possible, ensuring every show runs flawlessly.
